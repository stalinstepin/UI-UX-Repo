This week there will also be POWER KIDS' CONFERENCE, COUPLES' RETREAT, DISCIPLESHIP RETREAT
12thINTERNATIONAL YOUTH CONFERENCE |23-28th JULY 2017



Previous Next

I write to welcome you for POWER 2017, the 12th INTERNATIONAL YOUTH CONFERENCE to be held here at Divine Retreat Centre from 23rd – 28th July 2017. The theme of the Conference is “I Have Come to Set the Earth on Fire” (Lk 12:49). During this week there will also be a discipleship retreat and special retreats for couples and children. Power 2017 will be a marvelous experience of the faith and the universal Church. Sessions will include inspiring talks, moving testimonies, vibrant gospel music, daily celebration of the Eucharist and Eucharistic adoration. The retreat will be led by Fr. Augustine Vallooran and the Divine team. Internationally renowned Catholic bishops, priests, preachers and musicians will be here to minister on these days. For this the 12th Power conference Cardinal Allenchery, Fr. Rob Galea, renowned Catholic priest and musician from Australia, Jude Antoine from Malaysia, Lalith Perera with the Risen Lord Community from Sri Lanka and Colin Calmiano from Bangalore will be here. The music ministries include the Divine music ministry, Empowered from Malaysia and FAITH from Goa. Groups of youth from different parts of the world will be here to journey together and experience the mighty outpouring of grace in these days.

Here are some details that would be helpful for you to prepare for the retreat.

Retreat Duration 23 July (From 10 am) to 28 July (1 pm)
Registration Fee Rs 400/- per person(includes accommodation in dormitory and regular cuisine meals)
Multi-cuisine charges Rs 1000/- per person (Less-spicy food)
Accommodation Youth would need to stay in the dormitories so that the sick, elderly, aged and sponsor card holders are given rooms.

Sponsor card holders must bring along their original sponsorship cards.

For families that opt for prior registration, rooms could be made available on payment basis

NOTE: Dorms and rooms will be cleaned on 21st July 2017 after the regular weekly retreat; hence accommodation for Power Conference participants is not possible before 8am on 22nd July 2017. Kindly plan your arrival accordingly.
Requirements Bible, pen, note book, bed sheets, toiletries, umbrella.
Dress Code Comfortable, modest clothing appropriate for a place of worship. Please avoid tight-fitting, sleeveless, transparent, above-the-knee and sub-waist trousers.
Registration Online Registration Form is in the attached document. Please email the filled form to dyregr.drc@gmail.com. Those who register online and make full payment by sending a demand draft favoring“ Director, Divine Youth” will be given preference and will have quick admission facilities on arrival at Divine.

Spot & prior registration counters will be open from 8am on 22nd July 2017.
Location All the retreats in English will be held in the English campus of the Divine Retreat Centre which is in Muringoor on the National Highway (NH 47). Nearest airport is Cochin airport, COK (16kms away). NH47 is well connected by rail and road.

  1. We have the Divine Nagar station at the entrance to the retreat centre. Few trains stop here on weekends.
  2. The next nearest railway station is Chalakudi which is 5 kms away.
  3. The other major station that would be well connected is Thrissur. From here you can either come by connecting trains or bus.

Please do email us for further queries.
Yours in Christ,

Linda Jose
