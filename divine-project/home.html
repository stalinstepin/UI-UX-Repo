POWER

Every tribe and tongue gather around the throne of God and worship Him... thousands of youth gather from across the nations for a week to experience the beauty of Jesus. It is a grand experience of the Church & of the faith with Catholic leaders, musicians and preachers from across the world coming in to share their inspiring message.

The International Youth Conference is held every year in the last week of July since the first Conference held in 2006. Groups of youth from different parts of the world gather to journey together and experience the mighty outpouring of grace during these days.
