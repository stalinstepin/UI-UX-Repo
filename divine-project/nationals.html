NATIONAL YOUTH RETREAT




The National Youth Retreat is held twice a year in the last week of May & December (the vacation time for students in India). From across the country youth come to spend a life-transforming week in the presence of God. Each day begins with lively praise & soulful worship. Through the day there are inspiring messages preached by the Divine team with bishops & preachers from across India. Each day ends with crowning moments of the Holy Mass & Eucharistic Adoration.

We have held so far 37 National Youth Retreats. These retreats are moments of great anointing and conversion in the lives of the young. We have seen that the youth who come for these programs go back as fruitful members of their community, living vibrant lives of service and proclamation for the Lord.
